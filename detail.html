About the clothes

Casual or casual clothing is clothing worn at leisure or recreation. There are many types of casual clothing, this is adapted to the place where we do these leisure or recreational activities. This casual dress emphasizes comfort and personal expression over the presentation and uniformity of one's dress, which includes jeans, t-shirts, etc.
There are several things to consider when choosing casual clothes, including:

a). Choose a practical design and adjust it to a place to relax. If you are relaxed at home, choose a model that is a bit loose, if you are relaxing on the beach, choose a neck model that is slightly open so it is not hot, if you are relaxed in the mountains, choose a model that is slightly closed so that cold air can be overcome.

b) Choose a material that is strong and absorbs sweat.

c). DevelopmentFashion

Along with the advancement of science and technology, fashion fashion is also growing rapidly, although sometimes the fashion is not in accordance with good dress procedures, but fashion continues to roll from time to time.
